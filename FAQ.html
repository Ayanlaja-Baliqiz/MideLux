FAQs

Got question?We've got answers.

  • What type of services does MideLux Hotel offer ?

    • MideLux Hotel offers luxury accomodation, room service, event hosting,a restaurantand a relaxation spaces for guests.

  • How do i book a room at MideLux Hotel?

    • You can book a room online through our website,by calling our receptionist or by sending a message on WhatsApp/Instagram.

  • Do you accept walk-in guests?

    • Yes. MideLux Hotel accepts both walk-in guests and online reservations.

  • What time is check-in and check-out?

    • Check-in is from 2:00 PM, and check-out is 12:00. Early check-in or late check-out can be requested.

  • Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

    • Yes. All guests enjoy free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

  • Is breakfast included in the room rate?

    • Some room packages include breakfast. Guests can also order from our restaurant.

  • Does MideLux have a restaurant or bar?

    • Yes. Our hotel has a restaurant and bar that serves local and continental dishes.

  • Is parking available?

    • Yes. We offer secure and spacious parking for all guests.

  • Do you host events or parties?

    • Yes. MideLux hotel is available for meetings, birthdays, small events and private gatherings.

  • Is the hotel safe and secure?

    • Yes. We provide 24/7 security, CCTV monitoring and trained security personnel.
